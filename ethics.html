IT Ethics and Developer Guidelines

Ethical technology development requires transparency, fairness, privacy protection, accountability, and user respect.

  • Protect user privacy and personal data
  • Design systems fairly without bias
  • Be transparent about data collection
  • Allow informed user consent
  • Reduce harmful manipulation
