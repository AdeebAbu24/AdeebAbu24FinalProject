Welcome

Social media algorithms shape what billions of users watch, read, and believe every day. This website explores how these systems work, the technology behind them, their benefits, ethical concerns, and recommendations for a safer digital future.

What Are Social Media Algorithms?

Social media algorithms are automated systems used by platforms like TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube to decide which content users see based on data and engagement.
